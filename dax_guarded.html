Guarded A5/B25 SMA20 Lead Signal (DAX, max 1x)

Guarded A5/B25/X40/Y15 SMA20 Lead on ^GDAXI (DAX (^GDAXI)). Recovery tiers still arm at -5% / -25%, but max leverage is capped at 1x on this tab (no 2x/3x exposure). Buy-and-hold reference: - CAGR, - max drawdown.

Strategy Rules

  • Base trend rule: hold 1x DAX exposure only when the index close is above the 20-day SMA. Otherwise hold cash/T-bills.
  • 2x recovery tier (capped at 1x on site): if the DAX is down 5% or more from its DAX closing high-water mark, the 2x tier is armed.
  • 3x recovery tier (capped at 1x on site): if the DAX is down 25% or more from its DAX closing high-water mark, the 3x tier is armed.
  • Lead guard: recovery leverage can run when close is at least 99.25% of SMA20, i.e. within 0.75% below SMA20 or above it.
  • Exit recovery: 2x resets after the DAX rises 40% from the tier entry close. 3x resets after the DAX rises 15% from the tier entry close.

The official signal uses the latest completed daily close and matches the backtest logic. The intraday signal is provisional and can use a delayed DAX quote through an API/proxy or a manually entered price.
