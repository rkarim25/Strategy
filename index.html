SMA200 ±3% Band + RSI>20 Exit 2x Signal

Computes the strategy state from S&P 500 daily closes: hold 2x exposure while the close is above the SMA200 +3% band, move to cash below the −3% band, and hold the prior state inside the band. The RSI>20 guard keeps the position invested through deeply oversold dips (RSI(14) < 20) instead of selling into the panic. An Octane strategy from the Excel sweep (≈13.3% CAGR / −41.5% max drawdown over the full 1950+ history at 0.10% cost).

Strategy Rules

  • Trend filter (±3% band): go long at 2x when the SPX close rises above SMA200 × 1.03 (upper band); exit to cash when it falls below SMA200 × 0.97 (lower band). Between the bands, hold the prior state (hysteresis — no whipsaw exits).
  • Fixed 2x leverage: hold a constant 2x while long — no distance scaling.
  • RSI>20 exit guard: if the band would flip to cash but RSI(14) < 20 (oversold), stay invested at 2x instead of selling into the dip.

The official signal uses the latest completed daily close and matches the backtest logic. The intraday signal is provisional and can use a delayed SPX quote through an API/proxy or a manually entered price.

Strategy Parameters

The SMA200 ±3% Band + RSI>20 Exit 2x strategy uses fixed parameters:

  • SMA window: 200 days
  • Band width: ±3% around SMA200
  • Leverage: fixed 2x while long, cash otherwise
  • RSI window: 14 days
  • RSI exit threshold: 20 (stay invested at 2x while RSI < 20 instead of selling into the dip)
  • Hysteresis: hold prior state in the neutral zone between bands

These parameters are fixed and not adjustable via sliders. This is one of the Octane strategies from the cross-asset Excel sweep (full 1950+ history at 0.10% trading cost) — see the Summary page.

Backtests use listed US ETP daily returns (SPY/SSO/UPRO) for same-calendar accuracy; the execution vehicle is 3USL.L (UCITS 3x S&P 500).

Strategy Rules

  • Base trend rule: hold 3x S&P exposure when the SPX close is above the 200-day SMA plus a 3% buffer (SMA200 × 1.03).

  • Execution vehicle: 3USL.L (UCITS 3x S&P 500). Backtested on US-listed SPY/SSO/UPRO for same-calendar accuracy.

SPX 3x Strategy Details

The SMA200 ±3% band mechanism creates a neutral zone that prevents whipsaw exits during normal pullbacks. The RSI(14) < 30 guard provides an additional safety exit when momentum collapses, even if price hasn't crossed the lower band. B3 and B4 add leverage scaling within the in-market state: B3 uses RSI zones (RSI≥70→3x, RSI≥55→2x, RSI≥40→1x), while B4 uses VIX levels (VIX<15→3x, VIX<22→2x, VIX<30→1x).

All strategies use the same SMA200 ±3% band and RSI>30 exit guard. The difference is only in what leverage is applied when in the market: B1 = fixed 3x, B2 = fixed 2x, B3 = RSI-scaled 1-3x, B4 = VIX-scaled 1-3x.
