# Strategies & classification

The trading logic and how strategies are graded. For engine parameters see [`backtesting.md`](backtesting.md).

## Guarded A5/B25 — the primary strategy

Used across all asset pages. Drawdown-armed leverage tiers with an SMA20 re-entry guard:

- **A5 trigger:** −5% drawdown from peak arms the **2x** recovery tier.
- **B25 trigger:** −25% drawdown arms the **3x** tier.
- **Recovery targets:** 2x tier exits after **+40%** from entry (X40); 3x tier exits after **+15%** (Y15).
- **SMA20 lead guard (0.75%):** re-entry only when price is ≥ 0.75% above the 20-day SMA.
- **Leverage cap:** non-US assets (FTSE 250, MSCI EM, Gold, etc.) are capped at 1x.

Implemented in `test_guarded_balanced_candidate.py` (`guarded_strategy_leverage`) and re-implemented
client-side in each `{slug}_guarded.js` for the live signal. The tiered DD-recovery variant lives in
`test_tiered_dd_recovery_guarded.py`. (Both are libraries despite the `test_` prefix.)

## Classification: Water / Octane / Stillwater

Computed in `build_strategy_results_excel.py` (`classify_strategy`, `classify_all`, `classify_stillwater`) by
direct metric comparison against the **Buy & Hold 1x** row (B&H rows are excluded, labelled "Benchmark").
Keep the Excel **Definitions** sheet in sync with this logic.

### Water — strictly dominates B&H 1x on no-regret terms
Improves on B&H 1x on CAGR or MaxDD **without sacrificing any** of: CAGR, MaxDD, Sharpe, Calmar, Sortino, Vol —
**and** is strictly better on at least one of {CAGR, MaxDD}. Water takes precedence over Octane if both qualify.

### Octane — more return, bounded risk & turnover
ALL of:
- CAGR > B&H 1x CAGR (strictly)
- Calmar > B&H 1x Calmar (strictly)
- MaxDD ≥ −45.0% (no worse than −45%)
- Trades/year ≤ 30

### Stillwater
A newer Nasdaq-focused class (added 2026-06). Tolerance `STILLWATER_CAGR_TOL = 2.0`; built by
`build_stillwater_sheet` / `classify_stillwater`. Lives on its own sheet in the workbook.

### Known structural facts (don't re-derive)
- **S&P 500:** 11 Water + 11 Octane families (C21 "lever-up" families were added to `build_strategies`).
- **Nasdaq 100:** structurally has **no** Water/Octane — its ~16.5% CAGR requires 2x, which breaches the
  −45% DD gate. Don't go hunting for them.

## The S&P 500 Octane strategy on `index.html`

`SMA200 ±3% Band + RSI>20 Exit 2x` (CAGR ≈ 13.6% / MaxDD ≈ −41.5% / Calmar ≈ 0.33, since 1950):

- **2×** when close > SMA200 × 1.03 (upper band).
- **Cash** when close < SMA200 × 0.97 (lower band) **AND** RSI(14) ≥ 20.
- **Stay 2×** when the band says cash but RSI(14) < 20 (don't sell into an oversold panic —
  `rsi_exit_filter_on_series` blocks the exit; it never *enters* on RSI).
- Hysteresis between the bands (hold the prior state).

Data generated by `backtest_spx_distance_scale.py` (`DEFAULT_SPEC`) →
`spx_distance_scale_site_data.json` + `spx_distance_scale_etp_returns.json`. The browser mirrors it via
`bandRsiLeverageSeries(rows, params)` with `DEFAULT_DISTANCE_SCALE =
{smaWindow:200, bandPct:0.03, rsiWindow:14, rsiExit:20, leverage:2, tradingCost:0.001, cashRate:0.04}`.
